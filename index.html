Cairo International Airport Museum

The Cairo International Airport Museum is a cultural landmark located within the Cairo International Airport in Egypt. It serves as a showcase of Egyptian history, culture, and heritage, offering travelers a glimpse into the country's rich past and vibrant present.

National Museum of Egyptian Civilization

The National Museum of Egyptian Civilization (NMEC) is a prominent cultural institution located in Cairo, Egypt. It serves as a comprehensive repository of Egypt's rich cultural heritage, showcasing artifacts and exhibits that span thousands of years of history.

The Grand Egyptian Museum

The Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) is one of the most ambitious and anticipated cultural projects in Egypt. Located near the Giza Pyramids, it is designed to be one of the largest archaeological museums in the world, covering over 490,000 square meters.

The Egyptian Museum

The Egyptian Museum, also known as the Museum of Egyptian Antiquities, is one of the most iconic cultural institutions in Egypt. Located in Cairo, it houses one of the world's most extensive collections of ancient Egyptian artifacts.

Sharm el Sheikh Museum

The Sharm El Sheikh Museum was envisioned as a cultural institution that would showcase the rich history and heritage of the Sinai Peninsula and the broader region. It was expected to feature exhibits on ancient Egyptian civilization, Bedouin culture, marine life, and the geological and ecological significance of the Sinai Peninsula.

Museum of Islamic Art in Cairo

The Museum of Islamic Art in Cairo is a renowned cultural institution dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich artistic and cultural heritage of the Islamic world. Located in Cairo, Egypt, the museum houses one of the most comprehensive collections of Islamic art in the world.

The Coptic Museum

The Coptic Museum, located in Cairo, Egypt, is a prominent cultural institution dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich heritage of Coptic Christianity in Egypt. Established in 1908, the museum is housed in a beautiful building adjacent to the Babylon Fortress, an ancient Roman fortress in Cairo.
